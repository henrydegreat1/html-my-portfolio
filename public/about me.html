About Me

Henry Maduabuchi Okporozuo is a Nigerian Digital Marketing Specialist, SEO Expert, and Web Developer with a strong passion for technology, real estate, and social impact. He is fluent in English, Igbo, and Hausa, enabling him to communicate effectively with diverse audiences across Nigeria.

Henry currently serves as the Digital Marketing Manager at Residar Limited, where he spearheads high-performing marketing strategies, leads webinars, and promotes real estate investment opportunities such as Palm Haven and Ibadan real estate prospects. He also manages and designed the Teta Homes website (tetahomes.com) and oversees their blog on Nigerian real estate and infrastructure.

With over a year of hands-on SEO experience, Henry is skilled in on-page optimization, technical SEO, keyword research, analytics, PPC, content marketing, email marketing, and WordPress management. He uses SEO tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, MOZ, Google Keyword Planner, and Rank Math. He holds certifications from Google, Coursera, HubSpot Academy, and Udemy in Digital Marketing, Search Ads, and SEO.

In addition to digital marketing, Henry is an evolving Web Developer currently enrolled in a 100-day coding bootcamp for both web development and Python programming. He builds WordPress sites, custom landing pages, and functional websites for brands — including new ventures like ClickHive Digital and his upcoming laundry business platform that will handle online ordering, payments, and app notifications.

Henry has taught web design at youth trainings in his community and frequently speaks at church and community events. He has delivered presentations on social media marketing, lead conversion, and youth empowerment. In church, he has also taken roles such as delivering Youth Sunday sermons and contributing insightful questions during Bible studies.

His entrepreneurial drive spans real estate, tech, and content creation. Henry runs a political Facebook page with over 6,000 followers — mostly Northern Nigerian Muslims — and leverages the audience for affiliate marketing opportunities. He is also building WakaTheWorld, a project tied to travel and exploration themes.

As a real estate professional, Henry helps investors discover profitable opportunities in Nigeria. He writes newsletters, hosts FAQ sessions, creates video content, and guides aspiring agents on accessing and selling properties. His communications are known for their clarity, depth, and Nigeria-specific insights.

Henry holds a Bachelors degree from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, and continues to grow professionally in tech, real estate, and digital strategy.

Driven by excellence, creativity, and a desire to empower others, Henry strives to build solutions that make life easier, businesses more profitable, and investments more accessible to everyday Nigerians.
